About

Our mental health platform helps users monitor their stress and anxiety levels using advanced analysis of heart rate variability (HRV), sleep patterns, and environmental factors like noise. By providing personalized insights into mental wellness, the platform empowers users to take control of their mental health. Additionally, we offer simple stress-relief games to reduce anxiety and connect users with licensed medical professionals for consultations and appointments. Our goal is to create a comprehensive, user-friendly solution that promotes mental well-being and provides timely support when needed.
